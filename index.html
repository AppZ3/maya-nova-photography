About Maya

Photographer.
Storyteller.
Observer.

Maya Nova is a photographer with an eye for the space between moments -- the quiet breath before laughter, the fleeting geometry of light on skin. Her work lives in the emotional register that most images miss.

Available for portrait sessions, editorial commissions, events, and commercial projects across Australia and internationally.
